Fire Door Inspections

Fire doors are life safety systems — not joinery items. FHASE provides structured inspection and reporting aligned with an FDIS-style methodology, focusing on functional integrity, closing performance, gaps, seals, signage and compartmentation interfaces.

Book a Fire Door Review
  • Condition & damage checks (leaf, frame, glazing where present)
  • Self-closer performance and latching
  • Perimeter gaps, seals, thresholds and ironmongery condition
  • Compartmentation interfaces (walls/frames/service penetrations)
  • Clear reporting with prioritised actions
